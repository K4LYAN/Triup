A judgment-free zone.

We started Tri-up because we believe mental clarity shouldn't come with a price tag. In a world that never stops talking, true listening is rare.

Whether you are dealing with anxiety, relationship issues, work stress, or just loneliness, our volunteers are here to lend an ear. No fees, no sign-ups, no records kept.

100% Free

We are a non-profit initiative. Talking to us will never cost you a cent.

Anonymous

We don't ask for your name. We don't record calls. Your privacy is paramount.

Compassionate

Our listeners are trained to provide support with empathy and respect.
